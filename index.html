WRIGHT CITY YOUTH COUNCIL

Empowering youth to lead, serve, and make a difference in Wright City.

Mission Statement

To connect Wright City youth with local government, further understand local issues, and to create civic leadership opportunities that enhance our sense of community.

Establishment

The Wright City Youth Council was established in early 2025 under the direction of Mayor Michelle Heiliger and founder Keith Vines II. Consisting of high school students in Wright City who are currently in grades 9-12, the council provides opportunities to advise on local issues, and encourages service to the city.

The Wright City Youth Council meets as needed.

Past Projects and Initiatives

In addition to participating in Wright City community events such as First Friday and working closely with the Wright City Food Pantry, the council is always working to coordinate more service projects benefiting our locality.
